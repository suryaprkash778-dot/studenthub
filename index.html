About NoteVerse

NoteVerse is a smart academic platform designed to simplify the way students access study materials. Instead of searching multiple websites, everything you need is available in one place.

This platform provides structured notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), and additional resources organized semester-wise, helping students save time and focus more on learning.

Our mission is to make studying easier, faster, and more efficient for every student.
